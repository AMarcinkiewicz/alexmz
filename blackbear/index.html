BlackBear: Full UI/UX Redesign

Blackbear.global is a Netherlands based startup focused on building a platform that allows young professionals and university students to complete business challenges for cash rewards. I spent roughly 5 months working with BlackBear to recreate their entire user interface and user experience.

Problem

The first iteration of BlackBear's platform was visually unappealing, complex to navigate, and full of UX pain points which led to poor user retention.

Solution

The solution to BlackBear's problem was to redesign the platform from the ground up. First, this required me to understand how their business operated and what objectives they wished to meet with their product. After I had conceptualized their vision, I conducted a variety of research, made concept sketches, created high-fidelity mockups, and used rapid prototyping to help the company launch a new iteration of their product.


Design Process

To create BlackBear's UI/UX redesign, I structured my design process as the following:

1) Empathize and understand users/target market

2) Conceptualize BlackBear's product and revenue model

3) Detail UX pain points + Define user and client needs

4) Create UX flow charts, concept sketches, and mockups

5) Prototype, Implement, and test solutions
