hello

My name is Alex Marcinkiewicz, I am a Digital Product Designer. I enjoy creating and contributing my insights to products that have clear objectives and serve a purpose.

I see myself as more of an intuitive designer, meaning that I use my strong sense of intuition to guide me throughout the vast number of decisions made during the creative process.

I have two major goals I wish to accomplish within product design. The first being to improve the quality of our relationships with digital devices.  Our devices are consuming our time and attention and I believe that we should be building products that serve us rather than consume us. The second goal of mine is to work within a team of designers who are extremely invested in creative problem solving and executing an idea to its full potential.

skills

Skills: Interface + Visual Design, User Experience + User Flows, Prototyping, Responsive Design, Interaction Design, Product Thinking, and Product Strategy.

I have a decent understanding of mobile and web development. I’m certainly not a talented programmer but I can operate HTML and CSS enough to develop this site.

Tools: My primary design tools consist of Figma and Affinity Designer.  If your team or company has not yet made the transition to Figma, I can work with other interface design tools such as Sketch, InVision Studio, or Adobe XD.
